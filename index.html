About

I am a Sr. Applied Scientist at the GenAI Innovation Center at Amazon Web Services, working on large language models, machine learning, and AI.

Previously, I was a Sr. Staff Research Scientist at Samsung SDS Research America, and worked at Moody's Analytics in San Francisco. I was a Simons Postdoctoral Fellow and Lecturer at the University of Texas at Austin (2015–2017), affiliated with the Simons Center on Communication, Information and Network Mathematics and the Wireless Networking & Communications Group.
