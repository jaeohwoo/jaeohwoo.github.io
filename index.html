01

About

I am a Senior Applied Scientist at the GenAI Innovation Center, Amazon Web Services, where I work on making large language models more reliable, better calibrated, and grounded in principled foundations.

My research sits at the intersection of deep learning theory and practice — developing information-theoretic and Bayesian approaches to understand how AI systems reason, quantify uncertainty, and learn efficiently from limited data.

Previously Sr. Staff Research Scientist at Samsung SDS Research America and Moody’s Analytics. Ph.D. Applied Mathematics from Yale, B.S. Mathematics from KAIST. Simons Postdoctoral Fellow at UT Austin.

Research Areas

01

LLM Reasoning & Self-Evaluation

Evaluating logical consistency, improving instruction-following, and enabling reliable self-assessment in large language models.

02

Uncertainty & Calibration

Bayesian and information-theoretic approaches for quantifying uncertainty, calibrating predictions, and efficient data acquisition.

03

Mathematical Foundations

Entropy inequalities, information theory, and combinatorics establishing rigorous foundations for learning and inference.
